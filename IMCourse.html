Year 1

3D Fundamentals
This module introduces students to basic digital 3D production workflow to create assets for interactive projects. They will practice on doing high-poly detail onto low-poly mapping, texturing, animation, real-time lighting and rendering.

Design Principles
This module introduces students to the basic elements and principles of design. They will practice visual communication and the aesthetic use of line, shape, form, color, texture.

×

Year 2

Applied Design
This module introduces students to design application through digital props and environment concept illustration. Being able to practice perspective drawing, constructive drawing, color rendering, and compositional design.

Experiental Design
This module exposes and introduce students to designing media content and applications for various platform. With the concept of designing extended reality (XR) products with heavy emphasis on the User Experience (UX)
