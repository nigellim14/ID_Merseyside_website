Merseyside Polytechnic Course

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

  1. Develop innovative IT solutions that increase business competitiveness & enhance quality of life.
  2. Focus on core computing skills in programming, networking, databases and operating systems
  3. You could even start your very own e-business with a Diploma in Information Technology (IT).

CYBERSECURITY & DIGITAL FORENSICS

  1. Cybersecurity is crucial to protect organisations from cyber attacks
  2. Build a strong foundation in basic IT and security through modules such as Cyber Security Fundamentals, Databases
  3. You will develop skills in the areas of networking infrastructure, software security, malware analysis and digital forensics.

IMMERSIVE MEDIA

  1. Get trained in sought-after areas such as User Experience and User Interface (UX/UI) design
  2. Build an industry-standard portfolio by the time you graduate
  3. Develop enjoyable games or extended reality experience using Augmented/Virtual/Mixed Realities(AR/VR/MR)
